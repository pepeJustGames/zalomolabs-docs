# Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2026

ZalomoLabs respects user privacy. This policy explains the high-level categories of data used by DumbNotes and related services.

## Information We May Collect

- account identity such as email, display name, user id, and avatar
- content users create, such as notes, notebooks, comments, reactions, reports, and support tickets
- membership and entitlement metadata
- storage usage metadata for connected storage features
- security, moderation, and abuse-prevention logs

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

- operate DumbNotes and ZalomoLabs services
- authenticate accounts
- provide notebooks, profiles, posts, comments, and memberships
- process support and bug reports
- enforce safety and security rules
- improve reliability and product experience

## Payments

Payment providers may process payment information. ZalomoLabs should not store raw card numbers in the app repository or client code.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers only when required to operate authentication, database, storage, payments, security, support, or moderation systems.

## Security

We use reasonable safeguards such as authenticated sessions, database rules, storage rules, and encrypted transport. No internet service can guarantee perfect security.

## Contact

Privacy questions may be sent to the public support contact listed in [CONTACT.md](CONTACT.md).
